The Best Novels According to Me

My top novels of all-time

The Nightingale

This is my favourite novel so far, I've read it three times and every time I read it I cried and lived in the novel. I am simply in love with it.

Death on the Nile

I read this novel years ago, I don't remember anything about it except that I loved it and was obsessed.

Midnight Flight

As you might have noticed, I don't remember the name but what I remember is me not being able to look down.

Rob3 Gram

It's a novel about drug abuse. I actually listened to its Audiobook, I haven't read it. It was a journey, I lived every details and struggle the main character lived, although I was so young while reading it.

Sane3 Elzalam

The best horror-historical novel EVER.

Nadia

I don't remember much about it. I only remember waiting impatiently for the next audio book episode to upload.

Ith ma

A novel about remembering how fun it was when you were young, an awesome book that I would read ever couple of years to remember how having fun felt like.
